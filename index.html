My journey into front-end development began with a simple curiosity about how websites worked. As a self-taught developer, I’ve always been fascinated by how design and functionality come together to create seamless user experiences. What started as a hobby soon turned into a passion, pushing me to dive deeper into web technologies.

Now, as a UI Developer at Maxpro Technologies Private Limited, I get to bring that passion to life. Every day, I work on building responsive and accessible web applications that people find intuitive and enjoyable to use. I’ve learned how important it is to blend creativity with technical skill, and I’m constantly exploring new ways to improve the projects I work on.

I’ve spent a lot of time mastering HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I’m always eager to learn more. Whether it’s picking up new frameworks or finding ways to write cleaner, more efficient code, I love the challenge of problem-solving. My journey is ongoing, and I’m excited about where it will take me next as I continue to grow and learn as a developer.
